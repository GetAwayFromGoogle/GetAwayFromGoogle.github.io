Threat Modeling

Now, the most important thing to take back your privacy is threat modeling. A threat model says who you hide from, how important it is
to hide said info, your conveience level, etc. This great guide from Cupwire shows how to threat model. I will do my best to paraphrase.
The difference between Privacy and being Anonymous is that being Anonymous means you don't want people to know you use Signal.
Privacy is they know you use it, but not knowing how.







If you want to hide from your friends/family, simply lock your devices. Hiding from compaines can be stuff like changing to Firefox
from Chrome, Startpage from Google, Linux from Windows. Hiding from hackers and stuff requires commitment like lying whenever legally possible, hardening
your phone and hardening your OS. The extreme is true anonymity. The Cupwire article goes more in depth.
