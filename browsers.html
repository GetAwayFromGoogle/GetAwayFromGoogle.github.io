Browsers

Desktop Browsers

Brave

Brave is a really good beginner browser. Some notable features are the fingerprinting blocker, third-party cookie blocker, tracker blocker, the need of no extensions, and how it is similar to Google Chrome (In usability). A downside is it relies on the open source Chromium, by Google.
Even though of this, I reccommend Brave.

Mozilla Firefox

Mozilla Firefox is a really good browser for privacy and security. Some notable features are the social media blocker, third-party cookie blocker, fingerprinting blocker, its customizability, and the vast about:config. A downside is the Mozilla Foundation is making questionable business practices in order to make money, but as a browser, Firefox is amazing.
